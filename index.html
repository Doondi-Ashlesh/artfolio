MEET THE ARTIST
THE COMIC CREATOR BEHIND THE CURTAIN

The artist, known for blending gritty realism with fantastical storylines, brings old-school comic traditions to life with every pen stroke. His work often reflects themes of rebellion, nostalgia, and cultural critique, reminiscent of golden-age storytelling with a punk edge.

Raised on classic rock and newspaper strips, his influences range from 70s garage bands to underground zines. Each panel he draws echoes a vintage tone — dark ink, bold lines, and weathered textures — inviting the viewer into his analogue world.

His recent work was featured at the Indie Comic Fest 2025, where fans praised the raw emotion and unfiltered narratives in his black-and-white graphic novel trilogy.
